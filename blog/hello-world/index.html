🐣 Hello World!

I've been wanting to launch this website for months, and I finally found a few spare hours to get the basics in place. There are so many things I'm working on and have created already that I want to share that I have been struggling without a proper home for it all.

Up until this point, I've been publishing videos to my personal social media channels for fun and with no real process or branding. As people other than my immediate circle have started to get interested in this work, I realized I need to get organized. So today's site launch is the first baby step towards that end.

As far as the video content that's out there, I'm figuring out how best to migrate it all to the Wizardhead branded channels over the coming days. To date I have only accumulated a couple hundred subscribers on YouTube-- I hope I can convince them to come over and subscribe to the new Wizardhead channel as well. If you are one of them, thanks for subscribing!

Something else I really wanted to accomplish by relaunching this website is to be able to have some pages to point people to who are interested in the details of the production process. I have been meaning to write some tutorial articles and explainer videos and now I have a place to put them.

Today I am launching this website very much as a work in progress and with the intent to grow and refine it. If you are waiting to hear back from me right now, the delay is probably due to my focusing on getting this all together and you'll be hearing from me soon now.
